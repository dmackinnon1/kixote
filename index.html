Kixote Puzzles

When a knight has traveled around a chessboard touching every square exactly once, it has complted a "knight's tour." A knight's tour has been completed on the 8x8 board below, and the numbers show the order in which the tour was completed. Some of the numbers have been hidden. Your task is to reveal the hidden numbers in the correct order to complete the tour.
